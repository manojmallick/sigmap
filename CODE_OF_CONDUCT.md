# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful community for all contributors and users of SigMap, regardless of background, identity, experience, or perspective.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members
- Crediting contributors appropriately

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Publishing private information without consent
- Sexually explicit or violent content
- Deliberate disruption of community spaces
- Spam or low-effort contributions

## Enforcement

Community members who violate this code of conduct may be subject to:

- A warning from maintainers
- Temporary or permanent ban from community spaces
- Removal of contributions

## Reporting

If you witness or experience a violation of this code of conduct, please report it to **zzphandyman@gmail.com** with:

- Description of the incident
- Who was involved
- When it occurred
- Any relevant links or context

All reports will be reviewed confidentially. We do not tolerate retaliation against reporters.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to:

- GitHub issues and pull requests
- GitHub Discussions
- Community chats and forums
- Project documentation and comments
- In-person events and meetings related to the project
